LOOKERS.

An Online Platform for Job Opportunities and Applications.

LOOKERS is a web-based platform for job seekers offering online job interviews. It was developed from the Web Programming course and aligned with the Graduate Learning Outcomes (SCPL) of the Informatics Engineering Education (PTI) program at UM.

This platform responds to the need for easier, contactless job interviews—especially during the Covid-19 pandemic—while remaining attractive, user-friendly, and consistent with standard job-vacancy websites.

  • What We Did
  • Laravel
  • PHP
  • MySQL
  • RIASEC

Key Features of Lookers for Companies:

1. Create new job postings.

2. View applicant submissions.

3. Accept or reject applications.

4. Upload Google Meet/Zoom links for accepted applicants.

Key Features of Lookers for Job Seekers:

1. Browse all available job listings.
2. Submit job applications.
3. Upload application documents.
4. Track applied jobs (accepted/rejected).
5. Receive Google Meet/Zoom links if accepted.
