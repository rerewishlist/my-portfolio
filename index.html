About Me

Hi, I’m Muhammad Raihansyah Firdaus. I am a passionate web and app designer who loves creating clean, functional and user-friendly digital experiences. With a background in web development and UI/UX design, I enjoy transforming complex ideas into simple, beautiful interfaces.

My interests range from developing full-stack web applications (Laravel, PHP) to designing intuitive mobile apps (Figma). I’m always eager to learn new technologies and collaborate on innovative projects.

In addition to my technical skills, I also have strong leadership and team coordination abilities. I served as the Chairperson of the Taekwondo Student Activity Unit at Universitas Negeri Malang (UM) in 2023, where I honed my organizational, communication, and teamwork skills through managing events, guiding members, and building a solid team culture.
