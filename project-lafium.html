LAFIUM.

Lost and Found in UM – Helping You Report and Recover Lost Items on UM.

LAFIUM is a Figma app design concept for Universitas Negeri Malang (UM) that allows students and staff to report lost or found items on campus quickly and easily. With a clean and intuitive interface, the app streamlines the process of posting, searching, and managing lost-and-found reports.

The design focuses on speed, transparency, and community support. By connecting people who have lost items with those who find them, LAFIUM creates a reliable, user-friendly platform to help the UM campus stay organized and supportive.
